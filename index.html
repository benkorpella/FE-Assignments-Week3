Welcome to Eddy's Crab Shack

Our Story

Welcome to Eddy's Crab Shack, where every dish tells a story of passion for seafood and a dedication to quality that spans generations.

Eddy's Crab Shack was founded in 1975 by Edward "Eddy" Thompson, a seasoned fisherman with a love for the ocean and its bounty. Growing up on the shores of a small coastal town, Eddy developed a deep appreciation for the freshest seafood and the art of cooking it to perfection.

In the early days, Eddy's Crab Shack was nothing more than a humble seafood stand on the pier, where Eddy would sell his freshly caught crabs and seafood specialties to locals and tourists alike. Word quickly spread about Eddy's delicious crab cakes, hearty chowders, and succulent crab legs, and soon, the shack became a beloved fixture in the community.

As the years passed, Eddy's Crab Shack grew in popularity, expanding its menu to include an array of mouthwatering crab dishes and seafood delights. Eddy's commitment to sourcing only the finest ingredients and preparing each dish with care and attention to detail earned him a loyal following and a reputation for excellence.

Today, Eddy's Crab Shack remains a cherished destination for seafood lovers from near and far. Whether you're craving a classic crab cake sandwich, a steaming bowl of crab bisque, or a plate of buttery crab legs, you'll find it all at Eddy's Crab Shack, where every bite tells a story of tradition, flavor, and the simple joys of coastal living.

Join us at Eddy's Crab Shack and experience the taste of tradition for yourself. From our family to yours, welcome aboard!
